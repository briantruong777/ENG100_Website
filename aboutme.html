About Me

My name is Brian Truong and I am a student at the University of Illinois majoring in Computer Science. I've always liked computers (and video games), but only after being programming leader on my school's robotics team, have I realized that I want to pursue a career in software.

Anything technology or engineering interests me, but software development especially attracts my curiosity. I like the idea of carefully crafting a system that can handle immense amounts input and create something useful or beautiful. I like that idea even more when other people are involved because working with people increases the fun.
